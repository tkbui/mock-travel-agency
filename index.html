Overview.

The team at Adventure Travel Agency (ATA) is a tight-knit group of passionate and experienced world travelers from all over the U.S. Together, we help you by recommending and thoroughly planning awesome trips, ensuring that the preparation leading up to your deserved time off is seamless and stress-free.

We provide an awesome range of travel packages, set with basic guidelines and options so you can easily narrow down the specifics and choices you need to make when planning a trip. If you're more a seasoned and adventurous traveler, we even provide a DIY package option to craft a trip best suited to your tastes. Best of all, we flexibly provide our consulting services across all U.S. timezones via email and video chat so that you won't need to sacrifice your own time and schedule for the sake of proper planning.

Why be overwhelmed by all the decisions you'd make alone when you can spend that time anticipating for when the first day of your trip finally comes?

Travel Packages.

Peruse our different travel package options and contact us to get a quote from our team. From there, we'll help craft a personalized itinerary for your lodging, meals, activities, and more. Plus, get a 15% discount for every 5 people on the same trip (including you) using our service!

  • Pebble
    • Duration: 1 week (1 locale max)
    • Intensity Level: Easy
    • Recommended Locales: Australia, Germany, Hawaii, Italy, Spain
    • Details: For the easygoing traveler who's looking for something slower-paced and relaxing. We'll make sure you're put up in comfortable, familiar lodging near top tourist attractions, as well as some of our secret favorites!
  • Stone
    • Duration: 1 to 2 weeks (1 locale max)
    • Intensity Level: Normal
    • Recommended Locales: England, Italy, Japan, South Korea, Vietnam
    • Details: For the traveler who's already had some experience abroad, or is looking for something a step up from our Pebble Package. Your experience will give you the comfort of a new traveler while also helping you try new things, experience local culture, and gently broaden your comfort zone.
  • Boulder
    • Duration: 2 weeks to 1 month (2 locales max)
    • Intensity Level: Advanced
    • Recommended Locales: Belize, Egypt, India, Japan, Thailand
    • Details: For the traveler who's looking for a slightly extended stay abroad. You'll experience new sights, sounds, smells, and tastes that the average traveler won't find on their own or through popular travel media. Plus, we'll make sure you'll stay somewhere beautiful and comfortable that is also unique and expressive of the local culture.
  • Diamond
    • Duration: 1 to 3 months (4 locales max)
    • Intensity Level: Extreme
    • Recommended Locales: Bali, Bora Bora, Dubai, Egypt, Myanmar
    • Details: Not for the faint-hearted. If you're open and eager to the most thrilling and engaging vacation ever though, this is for you! We'll share with you our top tips, activities, and restaurants to make your trip an incredibly mindblowing experience.
  • DIY *
    • Details: Mix-and-match different durations, intensity levels, and locales (based on your decided travel duration)! With this flexible package, you can make the most of your time and easily hit everything on your bucket list (and more!) with the help of the trusted experts here at ATA!
