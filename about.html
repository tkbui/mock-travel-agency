About Us & Our History.

In 2008, the Adventure Travel Agency's founders—Basil, Herb, and Sage—first met during a high school travel volunteer trip with Global Glimpse. For two weeks, they were in Constanza, Dominican Republic to teach English, fix the piping at a local village, and experience local food, shops, and scenery. After that, the three kept traveling together through personal leisurely trips or more travel volunteering opportunities with Worldpackers. After becoming seasoned travelers, they wanted to share their own advice and first-hand experiences to make traveling a smoother, less daunting, and stress-free time for others.

With this goal in mind, the three began their consulting services in 2015 locally for neighbors, coworkers, and family friends during their free time. After their business grew from satisifed customers' reviews, the three dedicated themselves full-time to the ATA so that they could help even more people with their traveling goals and dreams.

Now, Basil, Herb, and Sage have added a handful of their other experienced travelers to their team. Together, they have a collective expertise of many travel destinations and their best-kept secrets—all used to perfect the itineraries we craft for our customers!

Our Mission.

Here at ATA, our mission is to ensure:

  • You're secured safe, clean, and comfortable lodging
  • You're comfortable and familiar with any differences or culture shocks (e.g. transportation systems) in your travel destination
  • You know tips that only locals or familiar travelers would know that make your trip easier
  • You get the most out of your budget based on your travel destination and package
  • You have an amazing, well-rounded trip—you'll have great food, lodging, activities, and more!
