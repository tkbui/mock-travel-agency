Travel Destinations & Highlights.

If you're interested in our top travel destinations and their highlights, look no further! Below we've listed our favorite places to recommend and travel to ourselves.

Destination Highlights Snapshot
Egypt Egypt is a spectacular and wondrous place. For seasoned and adventurous tourists, we definitely think you should give it a try. The grand deserts, sculptures, architecture, and history is something you won't want to miss.
Italy Italy is easily one of our top recommendations, especially for beginner travelers. Here you'll see beautiful and historical architecture, eat delicious Italian food, and see some amazing and unique sights (such as Sage's favorite, the Colosseum)!
Japan Japan is definitely a favorite, especially if you want to experience delicious food at all price points, efficient and accessible public transportation, and a great mix between modern and ancient Asian architecture.
